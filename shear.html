shear.get_shear_stress(shear: float) → numpy.ndarray¶

Calculate the stress after applying a shear in \(y\) direction

The shear tensor is

\[\left[ \begin{array}{lll}\sigma_{x} & \tau_{x y} & \tau_{x z} \\ \tau_{y x} & \sigma_{y} & \tau_{y z} \\ \tau_{z x} & \tau_{z y} & \sigma_{z}\end{array} \right]\]

if the stress is only applied in the \(y\) direction, \(\tau_{x z}=\tau_{y z}=0\)

Parameters

shear (float) – shear

Returns

shear stress \(\tau_{x y}\) (eV/Å^3)

Return type

float
